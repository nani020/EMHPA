Get to know us

The Ethiopian Muslim Health Professionals Association (EMHPA) is a registered, not-for-profit, non-political, and non-governmental organization in Ethiopia representing Ethiopian Muslim medical and health professionals. EMHPA's mission is to provide a platform for these professionals in the areas of medical education and ethics, student camps, and humanitarian and medical relief. The association is committed to developing the highest standards of proficiency and ethical practice within the Muslim health professions and passionately advocates for the role, relevance, and value of the Islamic perspective in health care to the public, employers, and commissioners.
